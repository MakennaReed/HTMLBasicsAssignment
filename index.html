Chocolate Lava Cake

by James Martin

A messy but delicous chocolate treat!

Ingredients✨

  • 3 chocolate bars
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/8 cup cream
  • 200 ml of powdered sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup of flour
  • 8 tbsp of butter or 1/3 cup oil
  • 2 tbsp of cocoa extract

    • Steps✨

    1. In a medium bowl, mix all your dry ingredients together (sugar and flour).
    2. In a seperate mixing bowl mix all your wet ingredients (milk, cream, eggs, oil/butter and cocoa extract).
    3. Mix dry and wet mixes together thouroughly + preheat oven to 325 degrees farenheit.
    4. Pour mix into your pan, lined with baking sheets and put in the oven and bake for 20 minutes.
    5. Take out and carve a medium sized hole on the top, from that hole scoop out some cake inside, save the piece of cake you cut
    6. Fill the hole you just made with peices of your chocolate bar.
    7. Put the piece of cake you cut off back on top and put cake back in the oven for another 5-10 .
